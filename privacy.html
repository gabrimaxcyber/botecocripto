Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Boteco Cripto, operated by Gabriel Medeiros, collects, uses, and protects the information of users of the application.

1. Information We Collect

When using Boteco Cripto, we may collect the following information:

  • Registration data: full name and email address
  • Profile picture: image voluntarily uploaded by the user
  • Published content: posts, comments, and likes made in the app
  • Push notification token: identifier used to send push notifications
  • Usage data: information about how you use the application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account in the application
  • Display your profile and published content to other users
  • Send notifications about new trades and relevant content
  • Manage your access to the PRO plan
  • Improve the application experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Supabase: database platform used to store app data
  • Expo: service used for push notification delivery
  • When required by law: in response to legal or governmental requests

4. Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on the Supabase platform, with encryption in transit and at rest. We take technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disable push notifications at any time in your device settings

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon requesting account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except when required by legal obligations.

7. Children

Boteco Cripto is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not intentionally collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will remove such information immediately.

8. External Links

The application may contain links to external websites, such as Gate.io. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites and recommend that you read their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the application. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
